About Me

Hello! I'm Christoffer Sommerfeld, a dedicated video editor and digital media specialist with over 5 years of experience in the industry. My passion for storytelling through visuals sparked my journey, transforming into a rewarding career where I craft engaging and immersive content.

I have a keen focus on outdoor media, advertisements, and educational content, utilizing my expertise in editing, sound design, and graphics to create compelling narratives that resonate with audiences. My work reflects creativity, meticulous attention to detail, and a commitment to high-quality production standards, showcased in my award-winning commercials and impactful media campaigns.

As a former Creative Services Director at KGWN and a Digital Media Specialist at Eastman's Hunting TV, I have directed and produced content for national television, delivering high-quality work on time and within budget. I thrive on collaborating with clients and teams to bring ideas to life, enhancing brand visibility and engagement through unique visual storytelling.

When I'm not behind the camera or editing suite, I love capturing the beauty of nature through outdoor timelapses and watching the Kraken play. I'm always eager to expand my skills and collaborate on exciting new projects. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
